The Bottom Pane

The Processes tab contain two panes: the upper pane is always a process list and the bottom either shows the list of DLLs loaded into the process selected in the upper pane, or the list of operating system resource handles the process has open.
The view mode determines which information is shown in the bottom pane. To switch the view, use the View->Bottom Pane menu item or the Ctrl+L (DLL view) and Ctrl+H (handle view) accelerator keys.

If you are only interested in seeing the processes running on your system, you can hide the bottom pane by selecting View->Bottom Pane->Off. You can bring back the bottom pane by selecting View->Bottom Pane menu or typing Ctrl+L or Ctrl+H.
